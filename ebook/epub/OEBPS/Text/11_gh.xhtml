ghositārāma

masc. name of a monastery in Kosambī; lit. Ghosita's park [ghosita + ārāma] ✓

Grammar masc, comp
Meaning name of a monastery in Kosambī; lit. Ghosita's park
Construction ghosita + ārāma
Compound chaṭṭhī tappurisa (ghositassa + ārāma)
Commentary (MNa) ghosit'ārāme'ti ghositassa ārāme. ghositanāmakena kira seṭṭhinā so kārito, tasmā ghosit'ārāmo'ti vuccati.
Sanskrit ghoṣila + ārāma

ekaṃ samayaṃ sambahulā therā bhikkhū kosambiyaṃ viharanti ghosit'ārāme. tena kho pana samayena āyasmā khemako badarik'ārāme viharati ābādhiko dukkhito bāḷhagilāno.

SN 22.89 khemakasuttaṃ

evaṃ me sutaṃ, ekaṃ samayaṃ āyasmā ānando kosambiyaṃ viharati ghosit'ārāme. tatra kho āyasmā ānando bhikkhū āmantesi, āvuso bhikkhave'ti. āvuso'ti kho te bhikkhū āyasmato ānandassa paccassosuṃ.
